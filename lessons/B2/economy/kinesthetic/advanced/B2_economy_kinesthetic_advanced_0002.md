### Warm-Up Activity

**Description:** Students move around and interact physically related to economic inequality  
**Materials:** Space for movement, props if needed

### Main Activities


#### Activity 1: Input Activity

- **Learning Style Adaptation:** Role-plays and simulations
- **Cognitive Level:** Evaluate
- **Description:** Students receive information about economic inequality through kinesthetic modality
- **Time:** 10-15 minutes


#### Activity 2: Practice Activity

- **Learning Style Adaptation:** Physical games and activities
- **Cognitive Level:** Create
- **Description:** Students practice language related to economic inequality using kinesthetic approach
- **Time:** 15-20 minutes


#### Activity 3: Production Activity

- **Learning Style Adaptation:** Real-world tasks and projects
- **Cognitive Level:** Evaluate arguments critically
