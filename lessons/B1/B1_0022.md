# Fashion and Style

**Level:** B1 - Intermediate  
**Lesson ID:** B1_0022  
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Methodology:** Communicative Language Teaching (CLT) with Task-Based Learning

---

## Learning Objectives

- Students will be able to discuss fashion and style using appropriate vocabulary
- Students will practice asking and answering questions about the topic
- Students will express personal opinions and experiences related to the topic
- Students will improve fluency through extended speaking practice

---

## Can-Do Statements (CEFR)

- Understand main points of clear standard input on familiar matters
- Deal with most situations while traveling

---

## Grammar Focus

- Present perfect vs past simple
- Comparative and superlative adjectives
- Modal verbs (should, could, might)

## Warm-Up Activity

**Activity:** Think-Pair-Share  
**Time:** 5-7 minutes

**Description:** Students think about their experiences with fashion and style, discuss with a partner, then share with the class

**Instructions:**

1. Give students 1 minute to think individually
1. Pair students and have them discuss for 3 minutes
1. Invite volunteers to share interesting points with the class

## Discussion Questions

1. What do you think about fashion and style?
2. Can you describe your experience with fashion and style?
3. How important is fashion and style in your life?
4. What are the advantages of fashion and style?
5. Have you ever had a problem with fashion and style?
6. Would you recommend fashion and style to others? Why?
7. How has fashion and style changed over time?
8. What would you like to learn about fashion and style?

## Useful Expressions


### Expressing Opinion

- I think that...
- In my opinion...
- I believe...
- It seems to me that...

### Agreeing

- I agree with you
- That's a good point
- You're absolutely right
- I think so too

### Disagreeing

- I'm not sure about that
- I see what you mean, but...
- I don't really agree
- That's not how I see it

## Assessment Criteria

- **Accuracy:** Uses basic structures with reasonable accuracy
