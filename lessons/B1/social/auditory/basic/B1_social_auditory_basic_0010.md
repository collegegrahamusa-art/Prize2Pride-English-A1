# Helping Others

**Level:** B1 | **Category:** social  
**Learning Style:** Auditory Learners  
**Cognitive Level:** Basic/Foundational

## Lesson Structure

### Warm-Up Activity

**Activity:** Listen and Discuss  
**Description:** Students listen to audio about helping others and share reactions  
**Materials:** Audio recording or podcast excerpt

### Main Activities


#### Activity 1: Input Activity

- **Learning Style Adaptation:** Listening activities and podcasts
- **Cognitive Level:** Remember
- **Description:** Students receive information about helping others through auditory modality
- **Time:** 10-15 minutes


#### Activity 2: Practice Activity

- **Learning Style Adaptation:** Group discussions and debates
- **Cognitive Level:** Understand
- **Description:** Students practice language related to helping others using auditory approach
- **Time:** 15-20 minutes


#### Activity 3: Production Activity

- **Learning Style Adaptation:** Oral presentations
