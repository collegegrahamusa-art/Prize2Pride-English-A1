# Apologizing and Forgiving

**Level:** B1 - Intermediate  
**Lesson ID:** B1_0039  
**Duration:** 45-60 minutes  
**Methodology:** Communicative Language Teaching (CLT) with Task-Based Learning

---

## Learning Objectives

- Students will be able to discuss apologizing and forgiving using appropriate vocabulary
- Students will practice asking and answering questions about the topic
- Students will express personal opinions and experiences related to the topic
- Students will improve fluency through extended speaking practice

## Warm-Up Activity

**Activity:** Think-Pair-Share

## Discussion Questions

1. What do you think about apologizing and forgiving?
2. Can you describe your experience with apologizing and forgiving?
3. How important is apologizing and forgiving in your life?
4. What are the advantages of apologizing and forgiving?
5. Have you ever had a problem with apologizing and forgiving?
6. Would you recommend apologizing and forgiving to others? Why?
7. How has apologizing and forgiving changed over time?
8. What would you like to learn about apologizing and forgiving?

## Useful Expressions


### Expressing Opinion

- I think that...
- In my opinion...
- I believe...
- It seems to me that...

### Agreeing

- I agree with you
- That's a good point
- You're absolutely right
- I think so too

### Disagreeing

- I'm not sure about that
- I see what you mean, but...
- I don't really agree
- That's not how I see it
