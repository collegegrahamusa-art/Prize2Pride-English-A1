## Lesson Structure

### Warm-Up Activity

**Activity:** Listen and Discuss  
**Description:** Students listen to audio about internet safety and share reactions  
**Materials:** Audio recording or podcast excerpt

### Main Activities


#### Activity 1: Input Activity

- **Learning Style Adaptation:** Listening activities and podcasts
- **Cognitive Level:** Apply
- **Description:** Students receive information about internet safety through auditory modality
- **Time:** 10-15 minutes


#### Activity 2: Practice Activity

- **Learning Style Adaptation:** Group discussions and debates
- **Cognitive Level:** Analyze
- **Description:** Students practice language related to internet safety using auditory approach
- **Time:** 15-20 minutes


#### Activity 3: Production Activity

- **Learning Style Adaptation:** Oral presentations
- **Cognitive Level:** Apply language in new contexts
- **Description:** Students produce original content about internet safety demonstrating intermediate thinking
- **Time:** 15-20 minutes

### Discussion Prompts

1. How does internet safety affect people?
2. What are the advantages and disadvantages of internet safety?
3. How would you solve problems related to internet safety?
