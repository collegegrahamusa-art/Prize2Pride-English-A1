# Stakeholder Capitalism

**Level:** C1 - Advanced  
**Lesson ID:** C1_0038

## Discussion Questions

1. How would you characterize the discourse surrounding stakeholder capitalism?
2. What underlying assumptions shape our understanding of stakeholder capitalism?
3. To what extent is stakeholder capitalism a product of social construction?
4. How do power dynamics influence stakeholder capitalism?
5. What are the epistemological challenges in studying stakeholder capitalism?
6. How might interdisciplinary approaches enhance our grasp of stakeholder capitalism?
7. What are the implications of stakeholder capitalism for policy formulation?
8. How does stakeholder capitalism intersect with issues of equity and justice?
9. What paradigm shifts have occurred in thinking about stakeholder capitalism?
10. How can we reconcile competing frameworks for understanding stakeholder capitalism?
11. What are the long-term ramifications of current trends in stakeholder capitalism?
12. How does stakeholder capitalism challenge conventional wisdom?

## Useful Expressions


### Academic Discourse

- It is worth noting that...
- One might contend that...
- This raises the question of...
- It is incumbent upon us to consider...

### Nuanced Argument

- While it is true that..., one must also consider...
- This is not to say that..., rather...
- The extent to which... remains debatable
- This phenomenon can be attributed to...

### Synthesis

- Drawing these threads together...
- In synthesizing these perspectives...
- The convergence of these factors suggests...
- Taking a holistic view...
